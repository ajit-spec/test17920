Denmark is a Scandinavian country known for its high quality of life and beautiful landscapes.
The country consists of the Jutland Peninsula and numerous islands, with Copenhagen as its capital.
Here are 2 key facts about Denmark:

1. Denmark is a Scandinavian country consisting of the Jutland Peninsula and 443 named islands, with Copenhagen as its capital and largest city.

2. It consistently ranks among the happiest countries in the world according to the UN World Happiness Report and is known for its high quality of life, social welfare system, and concept of "hygge" (cozy contentment).
